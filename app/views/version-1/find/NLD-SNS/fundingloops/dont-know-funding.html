Don't know which funding

That's fine don't worry, we are going to assume you don't pay the levy.

This means your pay bill is less than £3 million. You pay 10% towards the cost of training and assessing your apprentice. We call this co-investment.

You need to:

  • agree a payment schedule with the training provider
  • pay them directly for the training

The government will pay the rest (90%) up to the funding band maximum. They’ll pay it directly to the training provider.

As an employer you can get additional funding if you employ fewer than 50 employee.

Other incentives payments include:

  • taking on 16-18 year old apprentices
  • employing apprentices who are care leavers
  • employing apprentices who are on an educational and health-care plan
